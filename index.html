Mingzhou Liu

I am a PhD student in computer science at Peking University, where I am advised by Prof. Yizhou Wang and co-advised by Assoc. Prof. Xinwei Sun. I obtained my bachelor's degree in electrical engineering from Zhiyuan Honors Program, Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My current research interest lies in the intersection of causality and machine learning. In particular, I focus on developing causal discovery theories, and utilizing the recovered causal knowledge to enhance the reliability and robustness of learning algorithms. I am also an active member of Math.SE and Stats.SE, where I have earned ~1.2k reputations.

My email: liumingzhou [at] stu.pku.edu.cn
